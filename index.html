Code Fashionably

Coding app to inspire young girls that coding can help them pursue their passions.

Click here to download the app

Code Fashionably is a coding app to give young girls the opportunity to explore coding in a fun and friendly environment, giving them the chance to express their fashion design skills while gaining some coding skills and interacting with other like-minded players. A lot of girls start out with a love of science and technology, but lose it somewhere along the way, thinking they can't do it. This app is designed specifically to help encourage young girls to believe in themselves. It gives the player the opportunity to learn how to code using various programming languages. The app will give the user the opportunity to dress a model and take her to work. The work options consisted of different tech careers such as a software engineer, programmer, technology associate and more. Another great thing about it is how it gives the player the option to communicate with other people using the app from different places who they can share their ideas with or get help which will encourage social interactions.
