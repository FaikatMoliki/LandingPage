myPeso

AR Currency Conversion App for Travelors

Download App

MyPeso is an Augmented Reality app that helps travelors in the Philippines know exactly what they are paying for in Pesos compared to their own
currency. All you have to do is hold your phone to a price tag and myPeso does all the work! MyPeso also shows you where shopping destinations
are and where to get your currency exchanged. Have fun and shop to your hearts content knowing exactly what youâ€™re paying!

myPeso Main Features

Find Shopping Destinations

Send Money

Convert Currency
Hold your phone up while in walking down the street! myPeso will find direct locations and routes to shopping destinations. You can narrow your search to food, clothing, etc! myPeso does the hard work for you!
Don't want to pay in cash? No problem, you can use myPeso to send money directly from the app! All you have to do is get the recipient's myPeso account number, it's that easy!
Don't know what you're paying? myPeso's main feature will convert currency for you. All you have to do is hold myPeso up to any price tag and it wil convert the price into your currency. It takes the stress out of shopping abroad!

Design process

The Design & Development of myPeso app



What do you like about traveling to the Philippines?
What are some things you had trouble with while in Manila?
What types of things do you like to do when you travel?
What would you be concerned about if you traveled to Manila?


I interviewed several different people with different experiences.
One person was born and raised in Manila,
one goes there very frequently , one visits every couple years,
and one person who has never been.
I wanted to get different perspectives on travel and on Manila.


I found that there was a great convern with safety in Manila.
Those who travel there know the safety concerns while
those who haven't been are very cautious.
I also found a great interest in shopping and finding directions.

This app's goal is to use Augmented Reality to better
the user experience while Traveling to Manila, Philippines


While discussing in a group one of the major themes that
came about was language barriers.
I wanted to focus on a more unique issue to Manila.
I looked over some of my interviews and saw that there was a concern with Philippine currency.
Pesos value fluxuates very rapidly making it
difficult to know what you are paying.


1. The User can convert currency by simply holding their phone up to the price tag.

2. The User can convert metric units to imperial units simply by holding their phone up to the unit.

3. The User can find their way to a specific location by holding their phone up to find a route.

4. The User could see if their water was safe by holding their phone up to the container and letting the app examine it.

5. The User could see if a route was safe by holding their phone up and a checkmark or X would appear.



The ultimate goal was to create features that would help the user be safe and make safe decisions while in Manila.

I wanted to create a modern and ease of access interface. I wanted muted but fun colors with rounded icons and a sans-serif font.
